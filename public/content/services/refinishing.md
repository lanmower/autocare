# Concours-Level Refinishing

Using premium paint systems and meticulous preparation, our refinishing department achieves flawless results. Our climate-controlled spray booth and color-matching technology ensure perfect finishes that stand the test of time.

## Our Refinishing Process

### Surface Preparation
- **Complete Disassembly**: All trim, glass, and hardware removed for complete access
- **Body Work**: Any dents, scratches, or imperfections are meticulously repaired
- **Multiple Sanding Stages**: Progressive grits ensure smooth, uniform surfaces
- **Primer Application**: High-quality primer systems for optimal paint adhesion

### Color Matching and Paint Systems
- **Advanced Color Matching**: Computer-aided color matching for perfect results
- **Premium Paint Systems**: Only the finest automotive paint systems are used
- **Multiple Basecoat Layers**: Proper coverage and color depth
- **UV-Resistant Clearcoat**: Long-lasting protection against fading and weathering

### Climate-Controlled Environment
- **Spray Booth Conditions**: Controlled temperature, humidity, and airflow
- **Dust-Free Environment**: HEPA filtration ensures contamination-free finishes
- **Consistent Results**: Controlled conditions guarantee uniform application
- **Proper Curing**: Optimal temperature and time for complete paint cure

## Finish Options

### Concours Restoration
- **Show Quality**: Mirror-like finish suitable for concours competition
- **Historical Accuracy**: Period-correct paint systems and application techniques
- **Multiple Wet Sanding**: Progressive polishing for ultimate smoothness
- **Final Inspection**: Detailed quality control before delivery

### Modern Refinishing
- **Updated Paint Systems**: Modern durability with classic appearance
- **Enhanced Protection**: Advanced UV and chemical resistance
- **Color Variations**: Custom colors while maintaining authenticity
- **Easy Maintenance**: Finishes designed for regular use and care

### Specialty Finishes
- **Two-Tone Applications**: Complex masking and multiple color coordination
- **Metallic and Pearl**: Special effect paints requiring expert application
- **Matte and Satin**: Alternative finish textures for unique appearances
- **Racing Finishes**: Lightweight, durable coatings for competition vehicles

## Quality Standards

- **Multiple Inspection Points**: Quality checks throughout the entire process
- **Color Approval**: Customer approval before final application
- **Warranty Coverage**: Comprehensive warranty on all refinishing work
- **Long-Term Durability**: Finishes designed to maintain their appearance for decades

Our refinishing department takes pride in delivering finishes that exceed the standards of the original factory, ensuring your vehicle looks better than the day it rolled off the production line.
