# Precision TIG/MIG Welding

Our certified welders use state-of-the-art TIG and MIG welding equipment for seamless, strong joints. We specialize in aluminum, stainless steel, and high-strength steel welding for structural and cosmetic applications.

## Welding Capabilities

### TIG Welding (Tungsten Inert Gas)
- **Aluminum Welding**: Precision welding for classic and modern aluminum body panels
- **Stainless Steel**: Perfect for exhaust systems and decorative trim
- **Thin Materials**: Exceptional control for delicate sheet metal work
- **Aesthetic Welds**: Beautiful, clean welds for visible applications

### MIG Welding (Metal Inert Gas)
- **High-Strength Steel**: Structural welding for frame and chassis work
- **Production Welding**: Efficient welding for larger repair areas
- **Thick Materials**: Excellent penetration for heavy-duty applications
- **Versatile Applications**: Suitable for most automotive welding needs

## Specialized Applications

### Structural Welding
- Frame rail repair and replacement
- Suspension mounting point restoration
- Roll cage fabrication and installation
- Chassis reinforcement work

### Cosmetic Welding
- Body panel seam welding
- Trim piece fabrication and repair
- Custom bracket manufacturing
- Invisible repair welding

### Material Expertise
- **Aluminum**: Lightweight panels and trim pieces
- **Stainless Steel**: Exhaust systems and decorative elements
- **High-Strength Steel**: Modern vehicle structural components
- **Mild Steel**: Traditional automotive applications

## Quality Assurance

- **Certified Welders**: All our welders are certified professionals
- **Quality Testing**: Visual and non-destructive testing of critical welds
- **Proper Preparation**: Meticulous cleaning and preparation of all surfaces
- **Controlled Environment**: Climate-controlled workspace for consistent results

Our welding services ensure that every joint is not only strong and durable but also meets the highest aesthetic standards for your restoration project.
